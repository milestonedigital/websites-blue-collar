## Context
A personal agency portfolio for Thiago Humberto, founder of a web design agency specializing in trade business websites (Blue Collar niche). The site must project authority, craftsmanship, and results. It showcases 42+ client projects.

## Selected Approach: **Bold Modernism — Dark Agency Aesthetic** (Option 3)

This approach best fits an agency portfolio because:
- Dark backgrounds make project screenshots pop with maximum contrast
- The bold, premium feel positions Thiago as a serious professional
- Animated stats and counters build immediate credibility
- The project grid with category filters makes 42+ projects navigable
- The blue accent aligns with the websitesbluecollar.com brand identity
